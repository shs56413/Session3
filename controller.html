Instructions

Each color within the color-coding system signifies
a distinct level of emotional intensity.
For each designated color, individuals are
encouraged to document the coping skills that are
most effective for managing emotions within those
specific situations. Each coping skill serves
as a focal point for discussion, facilitating a
comprehensive exploration of diverse scenarios
and their corresponding coping strategies.
