Instructions

The purpose of this intervention is to assist students
in practicing the process of reframing their thoughts
from a fixed mindset to a growth mindset. Counselors
and therapists will employ open-ended questions to
identify any fixed answers provided by the student
in a given situation and support them in adjusting
their perspective towards a growth-oriented response.
