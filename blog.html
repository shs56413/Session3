The Angular Approach

  • 1. Goals

    Set clear and achievable goals. Break down larger objectives into smaller, manageable tasks. This will make your goals less overwhelming and help you track your progress effectively.

  • 2. Strength And Weaknesses

    SWOT counseling can be beneficial for personal development, career planning, academic performance, and overall well-being. It provides individuals with valuable insights into their abilities and challenges, empowering them to take proactive steps toward self-improvement and achieving their goals.

  • 4. Socio-Emotional

    Through socio-emotional counseling, individuals gain insights into their thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. This increased self-awareness allows them to understand their strengths, weaknesses, and triggers, leading to personal growth and self-acceptance.
